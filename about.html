The Source Matters

ASRX is a healthcare access platform built on one principle: you deserve to know exactly what you're putting in your body and who's behind it.

Our Mission

We exist to simplify access to pharmaceutical-grade peptide programs by connecting people with licensed providers and trusted pharmacy partners — all coordinated through a single, transparent platform.

🎯

Access, Not Advice

ASRX is a coordination platform. We connect you with independently licensed healthcare providers who make all clinical decisions. We never diagnose, treat, or prescribe.

🔗

Vertically Integrated

We partner directly with FDA-registered domestic manufacturing facilities. This gives us visibility into every step of the supply chain — from formulation to your front door.

🔍

Radical Transparency

We believe you should know where your peptides come from. Every batch through our pharmacy partners undergoes independent third-party testing for purity and potency.

How ASRX Is Different

Most telehealth platforms are middlemen connecting you to unknown compounders. We take a different approach.

🏭

Trusted Manufacturing Partners

Our pharmacy partners operate FDA-registered domestic facilities following strict GMP protocols. We know exactly where every product comes from.

👨‍⚕️

Real Providers, Real Care

Licensed clinicians in your state review every case. No rubber-stamping. No algorithms making clinical decisions.

💰

Fair, Transparent Pricing

Our integrated supply chain means we can offer pharmaceutical-grade quality at fair prices — no hidden fees, no surprise bills, cancel anytime.

📋

Compliance First

We maintain strict separation between our coordination platform and clinical decision-making. HIPAA-compliant systems protect your data at every step.
