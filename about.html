The Source Matters.

ASRX is a healthcare access platform built on one principle: you deserve to know exactly what you're putting in your body and who's behind it. An AccessRx company.

Our Mission

We exist to simplify access to pharmaceutical-grade peptide programs by connecting people with licensed providers and trusted pharmacy partners — all coordinated through a single, transparent platform.

🎯

Access, Not Advice

ASRX is a coordination platform. We connect you with independently licensed healthcare providers who make all clinical decisions. We never diagnose, treat, or prescribe — we enable access.

🔗

Vertically Integrated

We partner directly with FDA-registered domestic manufacturing facilities. This gives us visibility and control over every step of the supply chain — from formulation to your front door.

🔍

Radical Transparency

We believe you should know where your peptides come from. Every batch through our pharmacy partners undergoes independent third-party testing for purity, potency, and sterility.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

🔬

Science Over Marketing

We lead with evidence, not hype. Every product and protocol we coordinate access to is backed by research and vetted by licensed clinicians. No miracle claims. No wellness buzzwords.

🏭

Quality Is Non-Negotiable

Pharmaceutical-grade means pharmaceutical-grade. Our domestic manufacturing partners follow strict GMP protocols, and every batch undergoes independent third-party testing.

💰

Fair, Transparent Pricing

Our integrated supply chain means we can offer pharmaceutical-grade quality at fair prices. No hidden fees. No surprise bills. No insurance hoops. What you see is what you pay.

📋

Compliance First

We maintain strict separation between our coordination platform and clinical decision-making. HIPAA-compliant systems protect your data. Licensed providers make all clinical decisions.
