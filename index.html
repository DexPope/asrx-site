FAQ

Questions We Hear Every Day

Straight answers — no fluff.

Is ASRX a licensed medical provider?
+
ASRX is a healthcare access and coordination platform. We connect you with board-certified, licensed physicians who review your profile and issue prescriptions within their licensed scope. We are not a clinical provider — your provider relationship is directly with the physician.
Do I need a prescription?
+
Yes — all protocols on ASRX require a valid prescription. A licensed provider reviews your intake assessment and issues a prescription before any treatment ships. This is what separates ASRX from unregulated supplement companies.
Is this legal in my state?
+
Yes. ASRX operates through a network of telehealth providers licensed in all 50 states. Every prescription is issued by a provider with active licensure in your state of residence, in full compliance with federal and state telehealth regulations.
What if my provider doesn't approve my request?
+
If a provider determines a protocol isn't appropriate for you based on your health profile, you won't be charged for the protocol. Our care team will reach out to discuss alternative options or next steps. Your safety always comes first.
How is ASRX different from my regular doctor?
+
Traditional primary care often deprioritizes optimization — peptide therapy, GLP-1s, and performance protocols are frequently outside the scope of what a GP will prescribe. ASRX connects you with providers who specialize in these therapies and understand them deeply.
How is my health data protected?
+
All patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored in HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. Your information is never sold or shared with third parties. Only your assigned provider and the care team have access to your health records.
How does delivery work?
+
Once your prescription is issued, your treatment is dispensed by a licensed pharmacy and shipped directly to your door in plain, unmarked packaging. Most shipments arrive within 3–5 business days. Temperature-sensitive medications are shipped with proper cold-chain packaging.
What does treatment cost?
+
Pricing varies by protocol — most start between $79–$299/month. You pay directly without insurance involvement, so there are no surprise bills or claim denials. Your pricing is transparent and set before you commit.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can pause or cancel your membership at any time from your member portal. If you cancel before your next billing cycle, you won't be charged. Active prescriptions will be honored through the end of your current supply.
Do you accept insurance?
+
ASRX is a direct-pay platform — we do not bill insurance. This keeps your costs transparent, your care private, and the process fast. Many patients find that paying directly is comparable to or less than their insurance copays for specialist visits.
